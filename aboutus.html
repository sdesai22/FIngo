About Us

Fingo was created as a solution for keeping your personal belongings and mostly credit cards, drivers licenses or ID with you when going anywhere. Our vision is making the modern day wallet where you can track it with built in tracking device rather than buying a third party tracker. Our name came from Finance on the Go to "FINGO". At fingo we believe in your best interest and making sure your finances are safe on the go!
